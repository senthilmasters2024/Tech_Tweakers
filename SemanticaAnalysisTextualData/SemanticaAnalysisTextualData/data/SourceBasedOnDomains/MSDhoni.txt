Player Profile: MS Dhoni

Full Name: Mahendra Singh DhoniDate of Birth: July 7, 1981Nationality: IndianSport: CricketPlaying Role: Wicketkeeper-BatsmanBatting Style: Right-handedBowling Style: Right-arm medium

Career Overview:MS Dhoni is widely regarded as one of the best captains and finishers in cricket. Making his international debut in 2004, he led India to major victories, including the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup, 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and 2013 ICC Champions Trophy. Known for his calm demeanor and tactical acumen, he played a vital role in India's rise in international cricket.

Major Achievements:

Captained India to victory in 2007 ICC T20 World Cup, 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and 2013 ICC Champions Trophy

Only captain to win all three major ICC tournaments

Most successful Indian captain in limited-overs cricket

One of the best finishers in ODIs with 10,000+ runs

Known for the iconic winning six in the 2011 World Cup final

Playing Style:Dhoni was known for his unorthodox yet effective batting technique. His ability to finish games under pressure and innovative wicketkeeping skills made him a standout player. His helicopter shot became legendary.
