Job Title: Healthcare Data Analyst/Medical Research Scientist

- Analyze healthcare data to provide insights on patient care, healthcare systems, and cost-effectiveness.
- Collaborate with doctors, nurses, and administrators to ensure data integrity and accuracy.
- Assist in the development of policies to improve the quality of care.
- Requires proficiency in data analysis tools such as R and SQL.
- Preferred background in healthcare management or public health.
-Conduct research on new treatments and pharmaceuticals.
- Work with clinical teams to design and implement experiments.
- Analyze data and prepare research papers for publication.
- Experience with clinical trials and understanding of medical ethics required.
- PhD in Medicine or Biological Sciences preferred.
